Why are events important?
Events precisely describe
how users want to use the software.
When you describe an application as a flow of events,
both the order of actions
and the characteristics of each action
are taken into account.
The technique of describing software with events is called
Event Storming.

Case study: Uber

Without event storming:

In order to do that, the user can open the app on his phone, then from the `order` menu, he can choose the place he wants to get to. Then, the app will read the phone's GPS address and find a route to the destination point. After that, the closest drivers will be notified that a new client is avialable. The first driver to click on the 'accept route' button will then deliver the client ...
